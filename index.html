The Clinical Triage Feedback Tool simulates real-world triage decision-making using the Australasian Triage Scale (ATS). It enables students to compare their decisions against best-practice standards and receive immediate, structured feedback.

  • Scenario-based triage simulations
  • Student vs. benchmark comparison
  • Formative feedback generation
  • Performance tracking for reflection

The Feedback Synthesiser helps educators and students generate clear, consistent, and constructive summaries of qualitative feedback. It supports professional communication and reflective learning aligned with educational standards.

  • AI-assisted feedback phrasing
  • Streamlined synthesis of comments
  • Improved clarity and tone consistency
  • Export options for LMS integration

  • Aligns with professional nursing standards
  • Ready for integration in simulation and LMS environments
